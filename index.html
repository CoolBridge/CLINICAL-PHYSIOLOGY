Available Courses

Echocardiography

Learn how to perform echocardiograms and interpret the results. Understand cardiac structure and function through ultrasound imaging.

Electrocardiography (ECG)

Master the skills to perform ECG tests and recognize normal and abnormal heart rhythms. Gain insights into diagnosing cardiac conditions.

Cardiac Stress Testing

Explore the methods of conducting stress tests to evaluate cardiac performance under stress. Learn to interpret the results for clinical decisions.

Doppler Ultrasound for Blood Flow Measurement

Discover how to utilize Doppler ultrasound to assess blood flow and detect vascular abnormalities. Understand its applications in cardiovascular health.

Angiography

Learn the techniques for angiography and its role in visualizing blood vessels. Understand the implications for diagnosing vascular diseases.

Holter Monitoring

Gain expertise in Holter monitoring to continuously assess heart rhythms over 24-48 hours. Learn how to analyze the collected data for arrhythmia detection.

Spirometry

Learn to perform and interpret spirometry tests for lung function assessment. Understand the significance of various lung volumes and capacities.

Pulmonary Function Testing (PFT)

Explore comprehensive pulmonary function tests to evaluate respiratory health. Learn to analyze results and diagnose pulmonary conditions.
